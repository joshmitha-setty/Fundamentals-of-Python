Sheela has three things in her bag. She wants to compute the area of 3 things but 3 things are in different shapes. 
The three things are in square shape, rectangular shape, and circular shape respectively. Write a program to help Sheela to calculate the area of different shapes. 
Sample Input: 
5
5
4
2.0
Sample Output: 
25
20
12.56

Solution:
s = int(input())
l = int(input())
b = int(input())
r = float(input())
aos = s*s
aor = l*b
aoc = (3.14)*r*r
print(aos)
print(aor)
print(%aoc)
